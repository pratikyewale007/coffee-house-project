About us

The Story of Coffee House:
Where Every Sip Tells a Story

Coffee House is a vibrant café founded in 2018 in Pune, known for serving premium coffee sourced globally and creating a warm, inviting space for everyone. With its first franchise in Kharadi and the prestigious "Time of Food Pune Award 2024", Coffee House has become a beloved destination for coffee enthusiasts. The café offers a relaxing atmosphere where customers can enjoy expertly brewed coffee, delicious pastries, and great conversation. Whether you're looking for a peaceful escape or a place to connect with friends, Coffee House promises an exceptional experience every time.
